The Manifesto

African builders are being archived wrong. The continent is in the middle of something historically significant and the documentation is shallow. You get the funding round. You get the valuation. You never get the person.

Nobody knew Rihanna when she was just a little gyal on the island. She got discovered. Docs Africa discovers the builders building Africa — and in doing so, discovers Africa.
